Festus Group Music Festival

FG Music Festival is an alternative music festival that brings the newest, most interesting and mind-blowing artists to Ghana. FG Music Festival will liberate you from your everyday surroundings we will welcome artists to Ghana, whom you might never even heard of, but have always wanted to discover. The artists who you will see are true to the music in their hearts, mainstream is not something they are drawn to. We will take a ride on the wild side and we are inviting all of you to join us to enjoy the freedom the independence. Get in touch with us!
